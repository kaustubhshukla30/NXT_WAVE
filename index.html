Slide 03 · Convert

The engine that compounds

Named tools doing named jobs, and a loop where each placement makes the next one cheaper.

What runs it

01

Clay + a research agent

Enriches accounts, scores ICP fit, detects the hiring and funding signals, verifies emails, and drafts the per-account opening line from the student's build and the company's product.

02

Apollo + LinkedIn Sales Navigator

Targeting and contact data for GCC engineering leaders, talent acquisition, and startup founders.

03

Instantly / Smartlead

Cold email infrastructure, domain warming, and the deliverability discipline that keeps the channel alive.

04

Close + Loom

Pipeline and follow-through, plus the 90-second build demo that does the actual selling.

Where AI-native is literal: agents do the research, scoring, and first-draft personalization at volume. Humans own the relationship and the close. That split is the point.

Why it compounds

Cold outreach gets the first placements. Those students ship real work, which becomes case studies and warm founder referrals, which make every next placement faster and cheaper. By months 5 and 6 the flywheel carries roughly a third of volume on its own.
